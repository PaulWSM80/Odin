Italian Pasta Salad

Description

An Italian pasta salad that's loaded with tri-colour rotini pasta, salami, bell peppers, and mozzarella. This is quick to make and full of flavour for a delicious summer meal.

Full details for this recipe can be found here.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salami
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until tender-yet-firm.
  2. Combine the pasta, salami, veggies, and mozzarella in a bowl.
  3. Toss in salad dressing, then stir in the dressing mix.
  4. Sprinkle with Parmesan before serving.
