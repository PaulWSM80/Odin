Lasagna

Description

A perfect homemade lasagna recipe that saves a lot of time. This recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short (and vague) ingredient list. Have fun!

Full details for this recipe can be found here.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasoning.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
